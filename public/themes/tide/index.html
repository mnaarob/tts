The story

Twenty miles from the boat to the bar.

We've spent six years building a route between the docks of Vancouver Island, the inlets of Howe Sound, and our raw bar. Most of what's on ice tonight was alive yesterday morning.

It means our menu is short, the chalk-board changes daily, and the answer to "what's good" is always: whatever was best when the boats came in.
