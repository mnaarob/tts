How we roast

A 12kg drum, three blocks away.

We roast in our own little workshop a few blocks from the cafe — a 12kg drum, by hand, in small batches. Most beans go from green to brewed in under 10 days.

If you ever want to come watch a roast, we run open-roast Saturdays once a month. Tickets are free; the coffee at the end isn't.

The relationships

Long contracts. Real prices.

Almost everything we roast is on a multi-year contract with the farm or co-op directly. We pay above the C-market price, every year, even when the market crashes — because the people growing the coffee don't get to ride out a bad market.

It's why our beans cost a little more than the supermarket bag. And it's why they're worth it.
