Our story

A small grocer with strong opinions about ingredients.

"We started Your Store Name because we wanted somewhere to buy the kind of olive oil we'd only had on holiday — and the kind of bread you can hear from across the room."

How we source.

We try to buy direct from growers and small producers within a 100-mile radius wherever the season allows. When something has to travel — your olive oil, your single-origin coffee — we go for one bottle, one estate, with the harvest date on the back. No house brands, no white-label.

If we wouldn't pour it into our own pasta water, it doesn't make the shelf.

What we believe.

Cooking should feel generous, not anxious. The right ingredient is half the recipe. Your shop should know your name. A loaf of bread is worth doing properly. Honey is a regional thing. The best meals are the ones you can repeat on a Tuesday.

When to find us.

Stop in any time during open hours — there's always coffee on for browsing, and someone behind the counter who knows where the lentils are.
